The Language Center is a newly built building with 7 rooms, including the staff room, and was made for students who have an interest in learning a different language. The Language Center has many new technologies, such as heaters, air-conditioning, a new window system and smartboards. It also provides computers and wi-fi access to students at Narrabundah.

Some of the technology include:

  • 10 Computers per room
  • Multiple Computers in the Hallways
  • Smart Boards in several classrooms
  • Wi-Fi Access
  • Laptop Trolleys in Two Classrooms
  • Air Conditioning
  • Heater
